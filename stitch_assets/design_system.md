# Design System: Obsidian & Ivory

## Overview
**Creative North Star: "The Obsidian Gallery"**
This design system is built upon the concept of "The Obsidian Gallery." We are not building a standard e-commerce store; we are designing a digital exhibition where the UI disappears to let the craftsmanship of the jewelry take center stage.

## Theme Details
- **Primary Color:** #FFFFFF
- **Background Color:** #131313
- **Color Mode:** DARK
- **Headline Font:** NOTO_SERIF
- **Body Font:** MANROPE

## Full Design Guidelines

### 1. Colors: Tonal Depth & The "No-Line" Rule
The palette is a sophisticated range of charcoals and deep blacks, punctuated by crisp, brilliant whites.

* **Primary (#ffffff):** Reserved exclusively for high-impact typography, primary CTAs, and essential highlights.
* **Surface Hierarchy:**
    * `surface`: #131313
    * `surface-container-low`: Used for large section backgrounds.
    * `surface-container-highest`: Used for interactive elements.
* **The "No-Line" Rule:** Direct sectioning with 1px solid borders is strictly prohibited. Boundaries must be defined through background color shifts.

### 2. Typography: Editorial Authority
The type system creates a rhythmic contrast between the timelessness of a serif and the modern precision of a sans-serif.

* **Display & Headline (Noto Serif):** Used for "The Hook." Should be set with generous tracking.
* **Title & Body (Manrope):** A clean, geometric sans-serif for technical balance.
* **Hierarchy:** Use `display-lg` for hero statements, `label-sm` in all-caps for categories.

### 3. Elevation & Depth: The Layering Principle
* **Tonal Layering:** Use background color shifts instead of shadows.
* **Glassmorphism:** Use `surface-bright` at 60% opacity with 20px `backdrop-blur` for floating elements.
* **Radii:** All corners are **0px**. Sharp geometry communicates precision and luxury.

### 4. Components
* **Buttons:** Sharp corners, solid primary or ghost border.
* **Inputs:** Underline-only style.
* **Cards:** No dividers. Use spacing or background shifts.
* **Jewelry-Specific:** Staggered grid for image galleries.
